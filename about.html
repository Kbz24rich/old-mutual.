About Old Mutual

Our rich history and leadership in financial services

Our History

Founded in 1845 in Cape Town, South Africa, Old Mutual has grown from a modest life insurance company into a leading international financial services group. Our journey began with the establishment of the Mutual Life Assurance Society of the Cape of Good Hope, created to serve the financial needs of the emerging middle class.

Throughout our 175+ year history, we've remained committed to our founding principle of mutuality - the idea that we succeed when our customers succeed. This philosophy has guided us through periods of significant growth and transformation, including our expansion across Africa and into other emerging markets.

Today, Old Mutual is a premium African financial services group that offers a broad spectrum of financial solutions to retail and corporate customers across key market segments in 14 countries.
